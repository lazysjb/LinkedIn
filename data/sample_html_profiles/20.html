Søren Andersen's Summary

With MAGMA since 1990 mostly in our US subsidiary doing sales, implementation, training, and support for our casting process simulation tool, MAGMASOFT. The other 5 years spent in Aachen, Germany primarily working on the development of MAGMAstress that is commonly used for prediction of residual stresses and distortions of castings and to resolve all kinds of cracking issues during casting and heat treatment processes.

Goal is to make "Solidification Modeling" as our tools are often called as common in the foundries as a furnace.

Specialties

Make steel castings right the first time through the use of simulation tools and experience with many different castings over the years.
